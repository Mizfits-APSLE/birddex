Kingfisher Birddex

We are Casper Tan, Jayden Lau, Zale Leow. We are the Velocirabirds, a group formed for the 41st Singapore Bird Race, which we got second place in. Through that, our passion for birdwatching blossomed. This is our Kingfisher Birddex. This will showcase our kingfisher images and information, while the ones we have yet to find will be added when we find them!
